About me

In my research I am interested in generative modeling in continuous and discrete space, curves in probability space and the application of stochastic processes in machine learning. Currently I'm junior professor at INSA Lyon within the PSBM team of Institute Camille Jordan. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at TU Berlin, in the team headed by Gabriele Steidl.

Open PhD position

I am currently looking for a motivated student for a fully funded PhD position. The research project will broadly focus on the intersection of stochastic processes and generative modeling in machine learning.
